### 3. Freenom (Free .tk, .ml, .ga) ⚠️
Freenom offers domains like `yourname.tk` for free.
- **How:** Visit [Freenom.com](https://www.freenom.com), search for a name, and checkout for 12 months (Free).
- **Warning:** These domains are often flagged as "low quality" by Google Ads, which might delay your AdSense approval.

### 4. How to Connect a Free Domain to Firebase
Once you get a domain from Namecheap or Freenom:
1. Go to [Firebase Hosting Console](https://console.firebase.google.com/project/studio-9489287013-59986/hosting/main).
2. Click **"Add Custom Domain"**.
3. Enter your domain (e.g., `videomaster.tk`).
4. Firebase will give you **A Records** (IP addresses).
5. Go to your domain provider's DNS settings and paste those IP addresses.
6. Wait 1-2 hours, and your free custom domain will be LIVE!

**Pro Tip:** For a professional AI business, the `.web.app` domain you currently have is much better for AdSense than a `.tk` or `.ml` domain.
