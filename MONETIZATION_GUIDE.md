# 💰 Why Haven't I Been Paid Yet? (The "Money" Protocol)

Bhaai, code 100% ready hai, lekin paisa aane ke liye Google aur Bank ke manual checks baaki hain. In steps ko complete kijiye aur paisa shuru ho jayega.

### 1. Google AdSense Approval (2-7 Days) ⏳
Maine ads jodh diye hain, lekin Google aapka domain `videomaster-ai.tech` check kar raha hai.
*   **Status**: Check karein [AdSense Sites](https://adsense.google.com/start/sites/).
*   **Problem**: Agar wahan "Getting Ready" likha hai, toh wait karein. Jab wahan "Ready" ho jayega, ads apne aap dikhne lagenge.

### 2. Subscription Payout (Razorpay Setup) 🏦
Aapka app ₹99 mang raha hai, lekin woh paisa kis account mein jayega?
*   **Action**: [Razorpay Dashboard](https://dashboard.razorpay.com) par jayein aur KYC poora karein.
*   **Step**: Ek "Payment Page" banayein aur uska link `src/app/premium/page.tsx` mein paste karein.

### 3. Google Search Console Verification 📈
Google ko bataiye ki ye app aapka hai taaki woh trust kare. Isse AdSense jaldi approve hota hai.
*   **Step 1**: [Search Console](https://search.google.com/search-console) par domain verify karein.
*   **Step 2**: Sitemaps mein `sitemap.xml` submit karein.

### 💸 Fast-Track Formula:
1.  **Terminal command**: Run `npm run web:deploy` (Isse aapki `app-ads.txt` live ho jayegi jo AdSense mangta hai).
2.  **Marketing**: App link ko YouTube Shorts par share karein. Jab real users aayenge, tabhi kamayi hogi.

**Sab kuch automatic hai, bas Google ke approval ka wait kijiye!** 🚀💸💎
