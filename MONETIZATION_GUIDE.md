
# 💰 VideoMaster AI: Paisa Kamane Ka Plan (Business Guide)

Aapka app ab ek "Money-Making Machine" banne ke liye taiyar hai. Maine code mein monetization (paisa kamane ka system) set kar diya hai. Niche padhein ki paise aapke bank mein kaise aayenge:

### 1. Subscription Model (SaaS)
- **Free Users**: Inhe 100 credits milte hain.
- **Pro Users**: ₹99 ka "Pro Plan" lene par unhe unlimited access milta hai.
- **Agency Users**: Teams ke liye ₹499 ka plan.

### 2. Ads Se Kaise Kamayein? (New Feature! ✅)
Maine app mein **Google AdSense** aur **AdMob** ka system fit kar diya hai:
- **Banner Ads**: Landing page aur Dashboard par ads automatically show honge.
- **Reward Ads**: Dashboard par maine "Watch Ad" button lagaya hai. User ad dekhega aur aapko har view ke paise milenge (User ko badle mein 10 credits milenge).
- **Multiple Companies**: Aap Google ke ilawa Facebook Audience Network ya Unity Ads bhi use kar sakte hain.

**AdSense Shuru Karne Ke Liye:**
1. [Google AdSense](https://adsense.google.com) par account banayein.
2. `src/app/layout.tsx` mein jahan `ca-pub-PLACEHOLDER` likha hai, wahan apni Publisher ID daalein.
3. Google aapki site approve karega aur ads apne aap aane lagenge.

### 3. Bank Mein Paise Kaise Aayenge?
- **From Ads**: Google AdSense/AdMob har mahine ki 21 tareek ko aapke bank account mein paise bhejta hai (Jab aapki kamai $100 ho jaye).
- **From Subscriptions**: 
  1. **Razorpay Account**: Ek [Razorpay](https://razorpay.com) account banayein.
  2. `premium/page.tsx` mein apna payment link jodein.
  3. User payment karega aur aap usey Pro access de denge.

### 4. SEO Traffic = Zyada PAISE 📈
Maine app mein ye keywords optimize kiye hain:
- "AI Video Generator India"
- "Viral Reels Maker"
Jab log ye Google par search karenge, wo aapke ads dekhenge aur aapki kamai hogi.

---

**Maine kya-kya set kar diya hai?**
- ✅ **Ad Reward System**: User free credits ke liye ad dekhega.
- ✅ **Universal Ad Slots**: Pure app mein ads dikhane ki jagah banayi hai.
- ✅ **Pricing Section**: Landing page par saaf plans dikh rahe hain.

Aapka app ab **READY-TO-EARN** hai! 🚀💰
