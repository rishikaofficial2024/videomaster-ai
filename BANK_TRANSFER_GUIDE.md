
# 🏦 Bank Account Connect: Paise Account Mein Kaise Ayenge?

Aapka app ab paise kamane ke liye taiyar hai. Paise aapke bank account tak pahunchane ke liye ye steps follow karein:

### 1. Google AdSense (Ads Ka Paisa) ✅
Google aapko har mahine ki 21-26 tarikh ke beech paise bhejta hai.
1. [Google AdSense](https://adsense.google.com) par login karein.
2. **Payments > Payment Info** par jayein.
3. Jab aapka balance **$10 (approx ₹800)** ho jayega, Google aapko PIN bhejega address verify karne ke liye.
4. Jab balance **$100 (approx ₹8,300)** ho jayega, aap apna **Bank Account (IFSC & SWIFT Code)** add kar sakte hain.
5. Paisa direct **Wire Transfer** ke zariye aapke bank mein aa jayega.

### 2. Subscription Ka Paisa (Pro Plan ₹99) ✅
User jab ₹99 ka plan lega, toh wo paisa aapke paas Razorpay ya Stripe ke zariye aayega.
1. [Razorpay](https://razorpay.com) par account banayein (India ke liye best hai).
2. Apna **Bank Account** aur **KYC** pura karein.
3. Razorpay se ek **Payment Link** banayein.
4. Is link ko `src/app/premium/page.tsx` mein button ke saath jod dein.
5. User pay karega, aur Razorpay T+2 din mein paisa aapke bank account mein bhej dega.

### 3. Zaroori Checklist (Kamyabi Ke Liye)
- [ ] **PAN Card & Aadhaar**: AdSense aur Razorpay dono ke liye zaroori hain.
- [ ] **SWIFT Code**: Apne bank se SWIFT code mangein (Wire transfer ke liye zaroori hai).
- [ ] **GST (Optional)**: Agar aapki kamai ₹20 lakh se zyada ho jati hai, tabhi GST zaroori hai.
