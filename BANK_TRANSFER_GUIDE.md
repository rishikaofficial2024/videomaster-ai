
# 🏦 Elite Bank Payout Guide: How to Get Paid

Your VideoMaster AI studio generates revenue through two primary streams. This guide explains how to track your balance and transfer it to your bank account.

### 1. Subscription Revenue (Razorpay) ✅
When a user pays ₹99 for the Pro Studio, the money is processed via **Razorpay**.
*   **Where to check**: Login to your [Razorpay Dashboard](https://dashboard.razorpay.com).
*   **How to withdraw**: 
    1.  Go to **Banking > Payouts**.
    2.  Ensure your **KYC** is completed (PAN Card & Bank Account proof required).
    3.  Money is automatically transferred to your bank account on a **T+2 basis** (e.g., Tuesday's sales hit your bank on Thursday).
*   **Action**: Create a "Payment Page" in Razorpay and paste the link in `src/app/premium/page.tsx`.

### 2. Ad Revenue (Google AdSense) ✅
When users watch ads for credits, earnings are stored in your **Google AdSense** account.
*   **Where to check**: Login to [Google AdSense](https://adsense.google.com).
*   **Payout Cycle**:
    1.  **Threshold**: You must reach **$10 (approx ₹800)** to trigger address verification (PIN).
    2.  **Withdrawal**: Once your balance reaches **$100 (approx ₹8,300)**, Google initiates a **Wire Transfer**.
    3.  **Timing**: Payments are sent between the **21st and 26th** of every month.
*   **Action**: Add your **IFSC and SWIFT Code** (from your bank) in the AdSense "Payments" section.

### 3. Financial Checklist for Success
- [ ] **Razorpay Account**: Active with KYC completed.
- [ ] **AdSense Domain Approval**: Ensure `videomaster-ai.tech` is marked as "Ready".
- [ ] **Bank Swift Code**: Obtain this from your bank (required for international AdSense transfers).
- [ ] **PAN Card**: Ensure your PAN card name matches your bank account name.
